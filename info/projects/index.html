Pokélab is an all-in-one supplementary application for Pokémon GO players. My friend Orkhan Nadirli and I created this app because there weren't any sleek, well designed apps that had all the information and tools to complete the players' gaming experience. Pokélab was very successful as our first app, getting over eight thousand downloads within the first month.
EatingClubs.xyz
date
2016
website
eatingclubs.xyz
A website where Princeton students can check information on all 11 of the eating clubs in one place, such as the following: Which eating clubs are open? When are they open? Is there a special theme or event? It is a dynamic website, so it can be accessed on computers and on smartphones (both iOS and Android). Both look great. Clean UI allows for information to be checked quickly, and the information can also be updated very easily by the eating clubs themselves through their accounts.
On Track
date
2015
website
devpost
Created at HackDartmouth, On Track is a simple cross platform app and web extension that allows a user (Leader) to broadcast their location to everyone who knows the name of the broadcast. It is as simple as the Leader opening the app on their phone and providing all followers with the name of their session. The potential use cases stretches to tour guides herding their groups from place to place, to road trips, to the sharing of locations between friends.
